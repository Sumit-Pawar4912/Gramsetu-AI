Pradhan Mantri MUDRA Yojana (PMMY)

Scheme Name: PM MUDRA Yojana
Benefits: Business loans without collateral
Loan Types:
- Shishu: Up to Rs 50,000 (new businesses)
- Kishor: Rs 50,001 to Rs 5 lakh (growing businesses)
- Tarun: Rs 5 lakh to Rs 10 lakh (established businesses)
Eligibility: Small business owners, shopkeepers, vendors, artisans
Documents Required: Aadhar Card, PAN Card, Business proof, Bank Statement
How to Apply: Visit any bank, MFI, or NBFC
Helpline: 1800-180-1111
Website: mudra.org.in
Special: No collateral required for Shishu loans
