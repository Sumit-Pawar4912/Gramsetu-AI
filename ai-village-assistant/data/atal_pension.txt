Atal Pension Yojana (APY)

Scheme Name: Atal Pension Yojana
Benefits: Guaranteed pension of Rs 1000 to Rs 5000 per month after age 60
Eligibility: Indian citizens aged 18-40 years with bank account
Contribution: Rs 42 to Rs 1454 per month depending on age and pension amount
Documents Required: Aadhar Card, Bank Account, Mobile Number
How to Apply: Visit bank branch or apply through net banking/mobile banking
Helpline: 1800-110-069
Website: npscra.nsdl.co.in
Special: Government contributes 50% for eligible subscribers
