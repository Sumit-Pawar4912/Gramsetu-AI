National Pension System (NPS)

Scheme Name: National Pension System
Benefits: Market-linked pension savings with tax benefits
Tax Benefit: Up to Rs 2 lakh deduction under Section 80C and 80CCD
Minimum Contribution: Rs 500 per month
Eligibility: Indian citizens aged 18-70 years
Documents Required: Aadhar Card, PAN Card, Bank Account, Passport Photo
How to Apply: Visit bank, post office, or online at enps.nsdl.com
Helpline: 1800-222-080
Website: npscra.nsdl.co.in
Types: Tier 1 (pension account) and Tier 2 (savings account)
