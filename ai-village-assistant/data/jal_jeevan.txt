Jal Jeevan Mission Har Ghar Jal Free Tap Water Rural Household
Category: Utility Water
Benefit: Free tap water connection to every rural household 55 liters per person per day
Eligibility: All rural households without tap water connection
How to Apply: Contact Gram Panchayat water committee
Documents: Aadhar card, residence proof
Helpline: 1916
Website: jaljeevanmission.gov.in
Features: 55 liters per person per day, village water committee manages supply, water quality testing every 6 months
