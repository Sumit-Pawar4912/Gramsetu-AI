Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PMJAY)

Scheme Name: Ayushman Bharat PMJAY
Benefits: Free health cover of Rs 5 lakh per family per year
Eligibility: Poor families listed in SECC 2011 data
Documents Required: Aadhar Card, Ration Card
How to Apply: Visit nearest empanelled hospital, meet Ayushman Mitra
Helpline Number: 14555 (Toll Free)
Status Check: mera.pmjay.gov.in
Coverage: 1500+ treatments and surgeries covered free
