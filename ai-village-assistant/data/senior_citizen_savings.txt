Senior Citizen Savings Scheme SCSS 8.2 Percent Interest
Category: Financial Pension
Benefit: 8.2 percent interest per year with quarterly payout for senior citizens
Eligibility: Citizens above 60 years retired defense personnel above 50
How to Apply: Post office or authorized banks
Documents: Age proof, Aadhar card, PAN card, retirement documents
Helpline: 1800-11-2011
Features: Maximum investment Rs 30 lakh, interest paid every quarter, 5 year tenure extendable by 3 years, tax benefit under 80C
