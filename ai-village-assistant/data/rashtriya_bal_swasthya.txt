Rashtriya Bal Swasthya Karyakram Child Health Screening Free
Category: Health Child
Benefit: Free health screening and treatment for children aged 0 to 18 years
Eligibility: All children from birth to 18 years
How to Apply: School health checkup or Anganwadi center mobile teams visit schools
Documents: Birth certificate, Aadhar card
Helpline: 104
Features: 4D screening Defects Deficiencies Diseases Developmental delays, free treatment for 30 health conditions
