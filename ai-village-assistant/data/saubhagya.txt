Saubhagya Scheme Free Electricity Connection Har Ghar Bijli
Category: Utility Energy
Benefit: Free electricity connection to all unelectrified rural and urban poor households
Eligibility: Unelectrified households in rural and urban poor areas
How to Apply: Contact nearest electricity department or DISCOM office
Documents: Aadhar card, residence proof
Helpline: 1800-166-6696
Features: Free connection with LED bulb wire and switch, solar pack for remote areas, BPL families get free wiring also
