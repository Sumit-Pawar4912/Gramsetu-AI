Janani Suraksha Yojana Safe Delivery Cash Incentive
Category: Health Women
Benefit: Rs 1400 cash for delivery in government hospital rural Rs 1000 urban
Eligibility: BPL pregnant women SC ST women for all deliveries
How to Apply: Register at nearest ASHA worker or government hospital
Documents: Aadhar card, BPL card, bank account
Helpline: 104
Features: Cash incentive for institutional delivery, ASHA worker gets Rs 600, free delivery and post-delivery care, reduces maternal mortality
