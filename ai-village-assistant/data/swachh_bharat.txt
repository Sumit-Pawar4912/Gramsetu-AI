Swachh Bharat Mission - Grameen (SBM-G)

Scheme Name: Swachh Bharat Mission Grameen
Benefits: Rs 12,000 subsidy for construction of household toilet
Eligibility: BPL families, SC/ST households without toilet
Documents Required: Aadhar Card, Bank Passbook, BPL Card
How to Apply: Apply through Gram Panchayat or online at sbm.gov.in
Helpline: 1969 (Toll Free)
Website: swachhbharatmission.gov.in
Special: Money directly transferred to bank account after toilet construction
