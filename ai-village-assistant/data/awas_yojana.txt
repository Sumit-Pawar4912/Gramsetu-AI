Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana Gramin (PMAY-G)

Scheme Name: PM Awas Yojana Gramin
Benefits: Rs 1.20 lakh for plain areas, Rs 1.30 lakh for hilly areas for house construction
Eligibility: Homeless families or those living in kutcha houses as per SECC 2011
Documents Required: Aadhar Card, Bank Passbook, BPL Card, Caste Certificate
How to Apply: Contact Gram Panchayat or Block Development Officer
Helpline Number: 1800116446 (Toll Free)
Status Check: pmayg.nic.in
Payment: Direct to bank account in installments
