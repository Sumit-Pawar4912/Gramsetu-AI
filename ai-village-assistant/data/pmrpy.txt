PM Rozgar Protsahan Yojana EPF Government Contribution Employment
Category: Employment
Benefit: Government pays 12 percent EPF contribution for 3 years for new employees
Eligibility: EPFO registered establishments hiring new workers earning below Rs 15000
How to Apply: Register on pmrpy.gov.in
Documents: EPFO registration, employee details
Helpline: 1800-11-2811
Features: Incentivizes employers to hire more workers, benefits workers getting formal employment
