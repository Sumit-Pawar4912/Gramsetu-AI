PM Krishi Sinchai Yojana Irrigation Subsidy
Category: Farmer
Benefit: Irrigation subsidy up to 55 percent for small farmers
Eligibility: All farmers with agricultural land
How to Apply: Contact local agriculture department or Krishi Vigyan Kendra
Documents: Aadhar card, land documents, bank account
Helpline: 1800-180-1551
Website: pmksy.gov.in
Features: Drip irrigation subsidy, sprinkler system subsidy, water conservation, Har Khet Ko Pani, More Crop Per Drop
