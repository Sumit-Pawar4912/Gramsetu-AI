Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MGNREGA)

Scheme Name: MGNREGA Job Card Scheme
Benefits: 100 days guaranteed wage employment per year per household
Wage: Rs 220-300 per day depending on state
Eligibility: Any rural household adult willing to do unskilled manual work
Documents Required: Aadhar Card, Ration Card, Passport Photo, Bank Passbook
How to Apply: Visit Gram Panchayat office and fill Form 1
Helpline Number: 1800111555 (Toll Free)
Status Check: nrega.nic.in
Job Card: Free Job Card given within 15 days of application
