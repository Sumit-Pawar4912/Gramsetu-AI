Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

Scheme Name: PM Fasal Bima Yojana
Benefits: Crop insurance coverage against natural disasters, pests, diseases
Premium: Only 2% for Kharif crops, 1.5% for Rabi crops
Eligibility: All farmers growing notified crops
Documents Required: Aadhar Card, Bank Passbook, Land Records, Sowing Certificate
How to Apply: Visit nearest bank, CSC center or apply on pmfby.gov.in
Helpline Number: 1800180551 (Toll Free)
Status Check: pmfby.gov.in > Application Status
Deadline: Apply before sowing season starts
