PM Kisan Maandhan Yojana Farmer Pension Rs 3000 Monthly
Category: Farmer Pension
Benefit: Rs 3000 per month pension after age 60
Eligibility: Small and marginal farmers aged 18 to 40 years
How to Apply: Visit nearest Common Service Centre CSC
Documents: Aadhar card, bank account, land records
Helpline: 1800-267-6888
Features: Minimum pension Rs 3000 per month, government matches contribution, family pension on death of farmer
