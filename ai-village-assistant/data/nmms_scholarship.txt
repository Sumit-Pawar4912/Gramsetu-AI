National Means Merit Scholarship NMMS Rs 12000 Per Year Poor Students
Category: Education
Benefit: Rs 12000 per year scholarship for class 9 to 12 students from poor families
Eligibility: Government school students family income below Rs 3.5 lakh score 55 percent in class 7
How to Apply: Through state education department exam conducted in November
Documents: Class 7 marksheet, income certificate, Aadhar card, bank account
Helpline: 0120-6619540
Features: Rs 1000 per month for 4 years, for low income students, state level exam, direct bank transfer
