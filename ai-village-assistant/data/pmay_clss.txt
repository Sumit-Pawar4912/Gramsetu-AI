PMAY Credit Linked Subsidy Scheme Home Loan Interest Subsidy
Category: Housing
Benefit: Interest subsidy up to Rs 6.5 lakh on home loan for EWS LIG MIG families
Eligibility: EWS income up to Rs 3 lakh LIG Rs 3 to 6 lakh MIG Rs 6 to 18 lakh
How to Apply: Apply through bank or housing finance company
Documents: Aadhar card, income proof, property documents, bank account
Helpline: 1800-116-446
Features: Interest subsidy credited to loan account, reduces EMI significantly, first time home buyers only
