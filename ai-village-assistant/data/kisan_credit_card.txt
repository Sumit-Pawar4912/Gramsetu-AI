Kisan Credit Card (KCC) Scheme

Scheme Name: Kisan Credit Card
Benefits: Short-term credit up to Rs 3 lakh at 7% interest rate (4% with subsidy)
Eligibility: All farmers, tenant farmers, sharecroppers, self-help groups
Documents Required: Aadhar Card, Land Records, Passport Photo, Bank Account
How to Apply: Visit nearest bank branch (SBI, PNB, Bank of Baroda etc.)
Helpline: 1800115526
Website: pmkisan.gov.in
Purpose: Buy seeds, fertilizers, pesticides, farm equipment
Special: No collateral needed for loans up to Rs 1.6 lakh
