Government Scholarship Schemes for Students

1. NSP National Scholarship Portal Schemes
Eligibility: SC/ST/OBC/Minority students studying in Class 1 to PhD
Benefits: Rs 1000 to Rs 20000 per year depending on class and category
Documents: Aadhar Card, Income Certificate, Caste Certificate, Marksheet, Bank Passbook
Apply: scholarships.gov.in
Helpline: 0120-6619540

2. Post Matric Scholarship
Eligibility: SC/ST students after Class 10
Benefits: Full tuition fee + maintenance allowance
Documents: Same as above
Deadline: October every year

3. Pre Matric Scholarship
Eligibility: SC/ST students Class 1 to 10
Benefits: Rs 150 to Rs 350 per month
Apply: scholarships.gov.in
