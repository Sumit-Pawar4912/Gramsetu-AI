Skill India Mission - PMKVY

Scheme Name: Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY)
Benefits:
- Free skill training in 300+ job roles
- Certificate recognized by industries
- Rs 8000 reward after successful training
- Job placement assistance
Eligibility: Indian youth aged 15-45 years
Documents Required: Aadhar Card, Bank Account, Educational certificates
How to Apply: Visit nearest Skill India center or register at skillindiadigital.gov.in
Helpline: 1800-123-9626 (Toll Free)
Website: skillindiadigital.gov.in
Courses: Electrician, Plumber, Computer, Tailoring, Beauty, Hospitality etc.
