Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana (PMUY)

Scheme Name: PM Ujjwala Yojana
Benefits: Free LPG connection to BPL women + First refill free + Stove free
Eligibility: Women from BPL families, SC/ST households, PMAY beneficiaries
Documents Required: Aadhar Card, BPL Ration Card, Bank Passbook, Passport Photo
How to Apply: Visit nearest LPG distributor (HP, Bharat, Indane gas)
Helpline: 1906 or 1800-266-6696 (Toll Free)
Website: pmuy.gov.in
Special: Over 9 crore connections given so far
