PM Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana

Scheme Name: PM Kisan Samman Nidhi
Benefits: Rs 6000 per year in 3 installments of Rs 2000 each
Eligibility: All small and marginal farmers with land up to 2 hectares
Documents Required: Aadhar Card, Bank Passbook, Land Records (Khasra/Khatauni), Mobile Number
How to Apply: Visit nearest CSC center or apply online at pmkisan.gov.in
Helpline Number: 155261 or 1800115526 (Toll Free)
Status Check: pmkisan.gov.in > Farmer Corner > Beneficiary Status
Installment Months: April, August, December
