PM SVAMITVA Yojana

Scheme Name: PM SVAMITVA Scheme
Benefits:
- Legal ownership document (property card) for rural houses
- Can use property as collateral for bank loans
- Reduces property disputes
Eligibility: Residents of rural areas living in abadi (residential) land
Documents Required: Aadhar Card, Existing land documents
How to Apply: Survey conducted by government using drones - no need to apply
Helpline: Contact Gram Panchayat
Website: svamitva.nic.in
Special: Drone survey done by government automatically
