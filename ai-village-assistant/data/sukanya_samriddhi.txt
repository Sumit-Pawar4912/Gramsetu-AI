Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana (SSY)

Scheme Name: Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana
Benefits: 8.2% interest rate per year, tax-free savings for girl child
Eligibility: Girl child below 10 years of age
Minimum Deposit: Rs 250 per year
Maximum Deposit: Rs 1.5 lakh per year
Documents Required: Girl child birth certificate, Parent Aadhar Card, Parent PAN Card
How to Apply: Visit Post Office or authorized bank (SBI, PNB etc.)
Maturity: Account matures when girl turns 21
Withdrawal: 50% allowed after girl turns 18 for education or marriage
Helpline: 18002666868
