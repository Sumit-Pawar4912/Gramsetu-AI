PM Surakshit Matritva Abhiyan Free Antenatal Checkup
Category: Health Women
Benefit: Free antenatal check-up on 9th of every month at government hospital
Eligibility: All pregnant women in second and third trimester
How to Apply: Visit nearest government health center on 9th of every month
Documents: Aadhar card, pregnancy card
Helpline: 104
Features: Free ultrasound blood tests doctor consultation, high-risk pregnancy identification, available at PHC CHC district hospitals
