ICDS Anganwadi Integrated Child Development Free Nutrition
Category: Child Nutrition Health
Benefit: Free nutrition health checkup pre-school education for children 0 to 6 years
Eligibility: Children 0 to 6 years pregnant women nursing mothers
How to Apply: Register at nearest Anganwadi center in your village
Documents: Birth certificate, Aadhar card
Helpline: 1800-11-8004
Features: Free nutritional food, immunization, health check-up, pre-school education, referral services, growth monitoring of children
