e-Shram Portal - Unorganized Workers Registration

Scheme Name: e-Shram Card
Benefits:
- Rs 2 lakh accident insurance (free)
- Rs 1 lakh partial disability insurance
- Priority in government schemes
- Identity card for unorganized workers
Eligibility: Unorganized workers aged 16-59 (construction workers, domestic workers, street vendors, farmers, fishermen etc.)
Documents Required: Aadhar Card, Bank Account, Mobile linked to Aadhar
How to Apply: Visit CSC center or register at eshram.gov.in
Helpline: 14434 (Toll Free)
Website: eshram.gov.in
Special: Completely free registration
