Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana Rs 6000 Maternity Benefit
Category: Women Health
Benefit: Rs 6000 cash benefit for first live birth in 3 installments direct bank transfer
Eligibility: Pregnant and lactating women for first child aged 18 plus years
How to Apply: Register at Anganwadi center within 150 days of pregnancy
Documents: Aadhar card, bank account, MCP card, pregnancy registration
Helpline: 1800-11-8004
Features: Rs 3000 on early registration, Rs 2000 after first antenatal check, Rs 1000 after child birth, direct bank transfer
