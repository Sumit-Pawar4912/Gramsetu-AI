Poshan Abhiyaan National Nutrition Mission Child Nutrition
Category: Health Women Child
Benefit: Free nutritional support for pregnant women and children under 6 years
Eligibility: Pregnant women, new mothers, children under 6 years
How to Apply: Register at nearest Anganwadi center
Documents: Aadhar card, birth certificate of child
Helpline: 1800-11-8004
Features: Free nutritional food supplements, growth monitoring, counseling, reduces stunting and wasting in children
