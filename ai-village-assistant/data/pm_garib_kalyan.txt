PM Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana (PMGKAY)

Scheme Name: PM Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana
Benefits: 5 kg free food grain (rice/wheat) per person per month
Eligibility: All NFSA beneficiaries with ration card (Antyodaya and Priority Household)
Documents Required: Ration Card, Aadhar Card
How to Apply: Visit nearest Fair Price Shop (Ration Shop) with ration card
Helpline: 1967 or 1800-345-XXXX (state specific)
Website: dfpd.gov.in
Special: Scheme extended till December 2028, covering 80 crore people
